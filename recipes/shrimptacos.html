Shrimp Tacos

Description:

Shrimp Tacos are a delicious and easy-to-make dish made from shrimp, tortillas, and a variety of toppings.
They are perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a fun meal to share with friends and family.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, toss the shrimp with the olive oil, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.

  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side.

  4. Warm the tortillas in the skillet or microwave.

  5. Divide the shrimp among the tortillas.

  6. Top with shredded cabbage, diced tomatoes, chopped cilantro, and a dollop of sour cream.

  7. Squeeze a lime wedge over each taco before serving.
