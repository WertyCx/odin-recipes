Beef Stroganoff

Description:

Beef Stroganoff is a classic Russian dish made from beef, mushrooms, and sour cream.
It is a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for a cold winter night or a special occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Egg noodles or rice for serving

Steps:

  1. Season the beef with salt and pepper.

  2. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium-high heat.

  3. Add the beef and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes per side.

  4. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

  5. Add the onion and mushrooms to the skillet and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

  6. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute.

  7. Return the beef to the skillet.

  8. Add the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, and Dijon mustard.

  9. Bring to a simmer and cook until the sauce is slightly thickened, about 5 minutes.

  10. Stir in the sour cream and cook until heated through, about 2 minutes.

  11. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  12. Serve the beef stroganoff over egg noodles or rice.
