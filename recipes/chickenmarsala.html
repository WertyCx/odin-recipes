Chicken Marsala

Description:

Chicken Marsala is an Italian-American dish made from chicken cutlets, mushrooms, and Marsala wine.
It is a delicious and easy-to-make recipe that is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth

Steps:

  1. Pound the chicken breasts to an even thickness.

  2. In a shallow dish, combine the flour, salt, pepper, and oregano.

  3. Dredge the chicken breasts in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.

  4. In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter with the olive oil over medium-high heat.

  5. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides and cooked through, about 5 minutes per side.

  6. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

  7. Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter to the skillet.

  8. Add the mushrooms and cook until browned and tender, about 5 minutes.

  9. Add the Marsala wine and chicken broth to the skillet.

  10. Bring to a simmer and cook until the sauce is slightly reduced, about 5 minutes.

  11. Return the chicken to the skillet and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

  12. Serve the chicken Marsala with the sauce spooned over the top.
