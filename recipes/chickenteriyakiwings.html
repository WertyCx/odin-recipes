Chicken Teriyaki Wings

Description:

Chicken Teriyaki Wings are a delicious and easy-to-make appetizer or main dish.
They are perfect for parties, game day, or a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. Place the chicken wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  3. Bake the wings for 30 minutes, turning halfway through.

  4. In a saucepan, combine the soy sauce, honey, garlic, and ginger.

  5. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat.

  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water.

  7. Add the cornstarch mixture to the saucepan and cook until the sauce thickens, about 2 minutes.

  8. Remove the wings from the oven and brush them with the teriyaki sauce.

  9. Return the wings to the oven and bake for an additional 10 minutes.

  10. Garnish the wings with sesame seeds and green onions before serving.
