Tater Tot Casserole

Description:

Tater Tot Casserole is a classic comfort food made from ground beef, vegetables, and tater tots.
It is a hearty and satisfying dish that is perfect for a cozy family dinner or a potluck gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag frozen tater tots
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef and onion over medium heat until the beef is browned and the onion is softened, about 5 minutes.

  3. Stir in the cream of mushroom soup and frozen mixed vegetables.

  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Transfer the beef mixture to a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  6. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the top.

  7. Arrange the tater tots in a single layer on top of the cheese.

  8. Bake the casserole in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the tater tots are golden brown and crispy.

  9. Let the casserole cool for a few minutes before serving.
